Industrial Coal

We specialize in the mining and supply of high-quality industrial coal products, serving diverse industrial applications with reliable energy solutions.

Our mining operations are strategically located in Kitai, Songea, Ruvuma, and West Wood, ensuring consistent supply and logistical efficiency. We offer a comprehensive range of coal types to meet various industrial requirements, from energy generation to manufacturing processes.

Our commitment to sustainable mining practices and quality control ensures that our clients receive premium coal products that deliver optimal performance and value.

Coal Types We Supply:

Cobbles: Large-sized coal pieces ideal for specific industrial applications requiring slow, consistent burn.
ROM (Run of Mine): Raw coal directly from our mining sites, suitable for various processing needs.
Crushed: Processed coal in smaller, uniform sizes for efficient combustion in industrial boilers.
PCAS (Pulverized Coal for Activated Carbon): Specially processed coal for activated carbon production and specialized applications.
