SUPPLY OF WASTEWOOD

LIK & LAMBERT CONSULTANTS also works in the supply of waste wood that are obtained as a source of energy to industries. We obtain our resources from genuine and reliable sources that help us to do in time deliveries and quality products to our customers.

What Can Wastewood Be Used For?

Material Recycling: Chipped or shredded wastewood can be used to make particleboard, mulch for gardens, animal bedding, or boiler fuel for industries.

Composting: Clean, untreated wood chips can be composted to create soil amendments.

SUPPLY OF WASTEWOOD

Wastewood is a general term for wood material that is discarded, leftover, or considered a by-product from various processes. It is wood that is no longer needed for its original purpose but still holds material value. It is a type of biomass.

USES OF WASTEWOOD

Energy Recovery (Biomass): This is a major use. Wastewood can be burned directly in specialized power plants or processed into densified wood pellets or briquettes for heating and electricity generation.
