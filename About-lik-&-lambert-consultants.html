LIK AND LAMBERT CONSULTANTS is a privately owned legal and business consultancy firm, established on June 9, 2016, under the Business Names Registration Act (Cap. 213) of Tanzania. Headquartered in the vibrant city of Arusha, we stand as a dynamic legal powerhouse committed to delivering exceptional and innovative solutions across diverse sectors.

Our firm is powered by a team of sharp, creative, and highly experienced legal professionals dedicated to providing strategic guidance and tailored services that meet the evolving needs of our clients. We take pride in being a locally registered company with a global outlook bridging professionalism, integrity, and excellence to ensure every client receives the highest standard of service.

For nearly a decade, LIK AND LAMBERT CONSULTANTS has been a trusted partner to individuals, institutions, and corporations in industries such as health, tourism, manufacturing, finance, energy, and banking. Our mission is to continuously set new benchmarks of quality, reliability, and innovation within the legal and consultancy landscape.
