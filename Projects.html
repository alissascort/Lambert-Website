Industrial Projects – Tanzania

Building long-term partnerships with major industrial clients across Arusha and Dar es Salaam

Strategic Industrial Partnerships

We have established long-term partnerships with major industrial clients across Arusha and Dar es Salaam. Our consultancy and supply services extend to renowned companies such as Arusha Redgold, A-Z Textile Mills, Red Earth, Sunflag Tanzania Ltd., Lake Cement, Backbone, and Sayona Industries.

Our primary role with these industries has been the supply of high-quality coal—a crucial resource for industrial operations. Through reliable sourcing, timely delivery, and professional consultancy on energy efficiency and industrial optimization, Lik & Lambert Consultants has helped these companies sustain their production capacities while ensuring cost-effectiveness and environmental compliance.

We also provide strategic consultancy to leading manufacturing brands such as DARSH Industries, Sunflag Bakery Ltd., and A-Z Industries, where our team advises on operational improvement, supply chain efficiency, and sustainable industrial practices.

Healthcare Projects

Delivering specialized consultancy and advanced medical technology solutions

Advanced Medical Solutions

Lik & Lambert Consultants is deeply involved in the healthcare sector, where we deliver specialized consultancy and advanced medical technology solutions.

We have successfully implemented the Nurse Call System—a state-of-the-art communication solution that enhances patient care and hospital efficiency—at Mount Meru Referral Hospital and AICC Hospital in Arusha.

Additionally, our company supplies a wide range of medical equipment and orthopedic products, including spine, trauma, arthroplasty, and arthroscopy products. We are authorized to promote, import, sell, and distribute these healthcare products, ensuring hospitals and medical facilities have access to the latest and most reliable technologies in patient treatment and surgical solutions.
